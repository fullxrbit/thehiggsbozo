## On what's expected of young scientists

In a world that is getting more globalized day after day, where artificial intelligence is getting better and more reliable, and where information is more available and immediate than ever, young scientists and prospective researchers are standing at the feet of a mountain that has never been so steep. Graduate education offerings have never before experienced such a decline, in a moment when the studentship is as competitive and educated as it has ever come. As a result, the bar keeps getting higher and higher, the pace keeps getting faster and faster, and students are expected to partake in more and more activities that "signal potential of exceptional research".
